LVGL Pro Editor offers flexible, royalty-free licensing options designed to scale from individual developers to large enterprises.

## Free Use

- **Community (non-commercial) use**: LVGL Pro Editor is free to use for personal, educational, and other non-commercial projects.
- **Evaluation**: It's also free to evaluate before commercial development, so you can fully try it out and make sure it fits your workflow.

## Commercial Development

Once actual commercial development starts, a paid license is required. Two options are available:

- **Product license**: One product, one license. Ideal for teams shipping a specific device or application.
- **Platform license**: For more advanced needs, such as covering multiple products, floating licenses, an on-premises license server, and other enterprise options.
