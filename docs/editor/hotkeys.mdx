## Inspector Mode

Enable **Inspector mode** from the [preview toolbar](./overview#the-preview-toolbar) to keep it on, or hold the <Kbd>Alt</Kbd> key while hovering over the preview to enable it temporarily. While it's active, you can:

- Visualize bounding box, padding, margins, and the extra click area
- Check size and position, and resize or reposition the element by dragging
- Perform measurements

The following actions work while Inspector mode is active (no need to keep <Kbd>Alt</Kbd> held once it's toggled on from the toolbar):
